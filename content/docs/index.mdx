> The easiest yet most powerful way to control your Philips Hue lights and devices with your Mac

**We Love Lights** is a macOS application that provides comprehensive control over your Philips Hue and Zigbee smart lighting ecosystem. Whether you're a casual user wanting quick light control or a smart home enthusiast building complex automations, We Love Lights offers the perfect balance of simplicity and power.

# Why We Love Lights?

We Love Lights brings your smart home to your Mac with native macOS integration, offering both quick menu bar access and a powerful Studio window for advanced management.

## Key Benefits

- **Dual Interface**: Choose between the convenient menu bar app for quick access or the Studio window for advanced control
- **Native macOS Experience**: Built specifically for macOS with SwiftUI, following Apple's design guidelines
- **Multi-Bridge Support**: Control multiple Philips Hue or Phoscon deCONZ bridges simultaneously
- **Remote Access**: Control your lights even when you're away from home
- **Advanced Automation**: Create sophisticated rules with motion sensors and switches
- **Shortcuts Integration**: Automate with macOS Shortcuts app and Siri

# System Requirements

To use We Love Lights, you'll need:

- **macOS 13.0 or later**
- **Compatible bridge**: Philips Hue Bridge or Phoscon deCONZ Bridge (e.g., ConBee)
- **Network connectivity**: WiFi or Ethernet connection to your bridge

<Callout>
Bluetooth-only Hue devices are not currently supported. You must have a bridge for We Love Lights to work.
</Callout>

## What Can You Do?

### For Everyday Use
- Control lights and devices from your menu bar
- Quickly adjust brightness and colors

### For Power Users

### For Automation Enthusiasts
- Integrate with macOS Shortcuts for powerful workflows
- Set up motion-activated lighting
- Create time-based scene changes
- Build multi-device coordinations
